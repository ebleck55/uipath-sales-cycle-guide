Key Decision Makers & Personas

Sales Cycle Stages

1. Discovery & Initial Contact

Identify key stakeholders, understand business challenges, and establish rapport with decision makers.

Key Activities

  • • Identify key personas and decision makers
  • • Understand current automation maturity
  • • Assess pain points and business challenges
  • • Map organizational structure and stakeholders

Key Questions

  • • What are your top 3 operational challenges?
  • • How do you measure operational efficiency?
  • • What manual processes consume most time?
  • • What automation have you tried before?

2. Technical Evaluation

Demonstrate UiPath capabilities through POCs and technical deep-dives with IT stakeholders.

Key Focus Areas

Technical Architecture:
  • • System integrations and APIs
  • • Security and compliance requirements
  • • Scalability and governance needs
Proof of Concept:
  • • Platform capabilities demonstration
  • • Integration assessment
  • • Performance and ROI metrics

3. Business Case & ROI

Build compelling business case with clear ROI metrics and executive support.

ROI Components

Cost Savings:

Reduced manual effort, fewer errors, improved efficiency

Revenue Impact:

Faster processing, improved customer experience

Risk Reduction:

Compliance automation, audit trails, consistency

4. Procurement & Contracting

Navigate procurement process and finalize contract terms and conditions.

Procurement Checklist

  • • Vendor qualification and security assessments
  • • Contract terms and licensing negotiations
  • • Compliance and regulatory requirements
  • • Implementation timeline and milestones

5. Implementation Planning

Plan successful deployment with proper change management and training.

Success Factors

Team & Training:
  • • Implementation team structure
  • • User training and enablement
  • • Change management strategy
Governance:
  • • Success metrics and KPIs
  • • Ongoing support model
  • • Scaling and expansion plans
